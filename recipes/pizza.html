Pizza

Description:

Delicious pizza with zesty pizza soft and freshly grated mozzarella cheese.

Ingredients

  • Crust
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  1. Pre-heat oven to 375 degrees
  2. Thaw crust
  3. Spread sauce on crust
  4. Spread cheese on top of sauc
  5. Bake for 25 minutes or until cheese is golden
